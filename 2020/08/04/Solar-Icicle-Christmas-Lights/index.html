ATTENTION OF THE ICICLE LIGHTS：

  1. Please CHECK THE ICICLE LIGHTS ARE NORMAL OR NOT firstly before you do the lights arrangement, making sure that the lights can work normally.

  2. The icicle lights are NOT CONNECTABLE. If you have any issues about the product, please ask us by “ask a question” in seller profile or send us an email through our customer email, we will give you a satisfactory solution.

  3. DO NOT CUT THE NYLON CABLE TIES! They are used to fix the icicle lights line.

  4. Please STRAIGHTEN THE DROPS at first before you hang up the lights. There is a natural curvature of the icicle lights wire, the drops are flexible, making sure that it can achieve the best look.

  5. Maybe there will be water into the plastic cover after rain, and the cover turn a little bit yellow, similar rusted, but it is not really rusted. Do not worry about it, it will work normally.

ATTENTION OF THE SOLAR PANEL：

  1. Please turn the little switch ON, and put the solar panel in full sun for 4-6 hours.

  2. Please make sure that the solar panel is exposed to direct sunlight, especially in cloudy days and winter.

  3. Please wipe the solar panel with a soft cloth regularly to make sure that the icicle Christmas lights outdoor can be charged enough to shine at night.

  4. Do NOT install the icicle Christmas lights outdoor solar panel under strong light, such as street lamps.

  5. This product is waterproof, but cannot be dropped in the water, please do NOT install the icicle Christmas lights outdoor in a low-lying place, especially the solar panel.
